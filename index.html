Tempus Facilities Maintenance

Welcome to Tempus Facilities Maintenance – your go-to solution for property maintenance and repairs. We specialize in serving property owners and management companies across the Puget Sound region. With a skilled team boasting over 30 years of experience, we offer a wide range of services, from drywall repair to plumbing and appliance installation. Our mission is to tackle challenges and delivering high-value solutions, big or small. We respect your time and complete tasks efficiently, allowing you to focus on what matters. Contact us today for expert assistance tailored to your needs.

About Us

Tempus Facilities Maintenance is a licensed general contractor that was founded by Alex Vehorn to provide quality facilities maintenance solutions to residential and commercial property managers. With an experienced background in all aspects of residential construction, Alex has been building and remodeling since his first solo home in 2003. As business demands have grown, we have acquired a variety of skilled technicians who have the abilities and drive to fix what’s broken, right now. Our goal is to offer immediate relief from your problems so that you can rest, assured.

From electrical repairs and carpentry work to painting and drywall repairs, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet your unique needs. At Tempus Facilities Maintenance, we take pride in our attention to detail and strive to deliver the highest quality workmanship on every project, big or small. Our goal is to help you enjoy a well-maintained home, while saving you time, money, and hassle.

Services

Property Management

We are your first responders for almost any tenant problem. We do electrical and plumbing past the wall to include: light, switch, and outlet replacement, faucet, valve and cartridge replacement, water heater replacement, drain clearing, toilet repairs and replacements, and appliance delivery and installation. Appliance deliverer won’t install? We will. Water heater ruptured? We will kill the leak and get a new one installed immediately. Garbage disposal pouring into kitchen cabinet? We will have the tenant jamming a new one before their guests arrive. Additionally, we do scheduled maintenance and comprehensive turnover work like painting, vinyl plank flooring, kitchen and bath remodeling, and detector requirements. For commercial tenants, we do lighting repairs and upgrades, service plumbing and drain clearing, roof leak locating and emergency repairs, and many others.

Homeowner

We offer homeowners the same services we provide to property managers, on a case-by-case basis. Your home is an important investment and an integral part of your life, and keeping it in working order is necessary to maintain the value. As a service organization, we believe in serving our customers as best they need, and sometimes that means recommending a different contractor. Our specialty is rentals, so ask us how we can facilitate your Airbnb or Vrbo needs, and keep your customers happy and your ratings high.

Nonprofit

We count numerous nonprofit organizations among our past and current customers, and we understand your needs may differ from for-profit enterprise. Whether your constraints are budgetary, security or other, we aim to provide discreet and skilled service. All of our technicians have passed background checks as a condition of employment. We take your client’s safety and comfort seriously.
